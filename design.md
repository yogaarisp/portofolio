## 1. Profil Utama
* **Nama:** Yoga Aris Purwanto

* **Tagline:** Bridging Infrastructure and Innovation.

## 2. Spesifikasi Teknis (Tech Stack)
* **Backend:** Laravel 11 (PHP 8.2+)
* **Frontend:** Tailwind CSS (Vite)
* **Interaktivitas:** Livewire 3 / Alpine.js
* **Database:** MySQL / MariaDB
* **Server Management:** aaPanel (VPS Hosting)

---

## 3. Keahlian Profesional (Core Competencies) - Updated Order

### IT Service & Hardware Support
* **Preventive & Corrective Maintenance:** Perawatan rutin dan perbaikan mendadak untuk uptime maksimal.
* **Hardware Troubleshooting & Repair:** Diagnosa dan perbaikan komponen (Motherboard, PSU, Keyboard, LCD).
* **System Performance Upgrade:** Optimalisasi hardware (SSD, RAM, Thermal Paste) & debloating sistem.
* **Data Recovery & Backup:** Penyelamatan data & manajemen backup berkala.
* **Operating System & Software Deployment:** Instalasi & konfigurasi OS (Windows/Linux) & standar keamanan.

### Software Development
* **Laravel:** MVC Architecture, RESTful API, Eloquent ORM.
* **Tailwind CSS:** Utility-First Styling, Responsive Design, Custom Themes.
* **Automation Testing:** E2E Testing (Cypress/Playwright), CI/CD.

### Infrastructure & Network
* **Mikrotik Routing:** Advanced Firewall, Bandwidth Management, VPN.
* **CCTV Systems:** IP Camera Deployment, NVR/DVR Configuration, Remote Monitoring.
* **Windows Server:** Active Directory, Group Policy, Virtualization (Hyper-V).

## 5. Panduan Visual (Design System)
* **Style:** Curved Modern (Border radius: 16px - 48px).
* **Color Palette:**
    *   Primary: Deep Teal (`#0D9488`)
    *   Surface: White Smoke / Slate Light (`#F8FAFC`)
* **Typography:** Manrope (Clean, Sans-serif).
* **Mobile Design:** Mobile-first approach dengan integrasi foto profil yang dominan dan navigasi yang ergonomis.
